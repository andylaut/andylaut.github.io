Pitch tips

Adapted from the Poynter Institute for Media Studies

The Exigence

What is your post's main idea, and what need does that idea meet? What is the conflict or tension youâ€™ll explore or the newsworthiness of the piece? Think of this as a specific statement about the story you wish to tell.

The Homework

Explain what you need to do in order to produce the post. What do you already know, and what do you need to find out? How will you find out?

A Time Peg

Give a reason to care and a sense of timeliness. You have to be able to answer the questions: So what? Why do this story now? Why should the audience care?

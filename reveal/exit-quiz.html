Study Guide

  1. When we design technical docs, we use typography and spacing to help users ______ what they need, _______ what they _________, and ________ on that ___________.

  1. This semester, we learned to write sentences featuring ________ nouns and ________ verbs.
  2. concrete, action
  1. The "known-new method" is a great way to make your writing flow. When you use the known-new method, you ______________.
  2. use some word or phrase from the previous sentence near the beginning of the next sentence

  2. theme words
  1. Many proposals respond to RFPs. "RFP" means "___________."
  2. request for proposals
  1. When writing proposals, pay close attention to the RFP's _______________.
  2. criteria for decision making
  1. IMRaD stands for _________, __________, ___________, and __________.
  2. introduction, methodology, results, discussion
  1. A rigorous methodology includes an explanation of _________________.
  2. what counts, how much it counts, and how you count it

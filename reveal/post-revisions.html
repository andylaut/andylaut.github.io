Post 1 draft feedback

Plenty of others have made similar args. How is yours different?

Emphasize what makes your argument distinct.

News peg

Attach your arg to current events.

Before

Protect your privacy online.

After

Facebook says the future is private, but it also just spent up to a billi on mind-reading tech.

Fresh angle

A new take on an evergreen argument.

Before

How to get organized.

After

I tried every productivity trick I could find. Nothing worked until I found this.

New audience

If a common arg has failed to resonate with your audience, emphasize audience.

Before

Now more than ever, we need your vote.

After

Young people can change the world. All they have to do is vote.

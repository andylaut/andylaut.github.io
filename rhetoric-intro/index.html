The Rhetorical Situation

Rhetoric

The art of persuasion. The ability to make people think, feel, or do what you want them to think, feel, or do.

The Rhetorical Situation

  • Exigence
  • Audience
  • Constraints

Exigence

The need. The thing about the world that ought to be different.

Audience

The group(s) of people who can address the exigence. The people who can change the thing that needs to be changed.

Constraints

The factors that limit your possible responses to the situation. Can be imposed by the exigence/audience or by choice.
